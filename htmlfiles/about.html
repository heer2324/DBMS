Who are We

We're not just another online electronics store. We're a team of tech enthusiasts who believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality, affordable electronics. Whether you're a seasoned techie or just dipping your toes into the world of gadgets, we're here to help you find exactly what you need.

Meet Our Team

We are a group of enthusiastic and dedicated Computer Science undergraduates studying at Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU) in Gandhinagar, India. As students passionate about technology and innovation, we have come together to collaborate on a project that forms a vital component of our curriculum, focusing on the subject of DataBase Management System. As Computer Science undergraduates at PDEU, we share a common aspiration to excel academically and make meaningful contributions to the field of technology. Through our collaboration on this project, we aim to deepen our understanding of DataBase Management System, hone our technical skills, and cultivate a spirit of innovation and inquiry. Beyond academic pursuits, we aspire to apply our knowledge and expertise to real-world challenges, making a positive impact on society and shaping the future of technology.
